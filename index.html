Towards a simpler and better crowd

My research for the ongoing PhD focus on how to populate a virtual scene easily with moving crowds.

Traditional crowd simulation methods mainly focus on how to generate convincing trajectories for each pedestrian. When it comes to simulate video games and film making, producing the pedestrians' body animation require additional works. Not to mention the time people need to spend on finding the best parameters for different scenarios with different algorithms.

The idea of my PhD research is to propose novel paradigms or methods of generating crowd motions which avoid the need of profund comprehension of various crowd simulation algorithms. Meanwhile, we would like to generate each pedestrian's animation.
